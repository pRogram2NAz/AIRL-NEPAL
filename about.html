About the Lab

Bridging the gap between scientific AI research and sustainable farming in Nepal

Our Mission & Vision

The Agri-Intelligence Research Lab (AIRL) Nepal was founded to introduce cutting-edge artificial intelligence, remote sensing, and precision engineering into agricultural ecosystems. We strive to empower smallholder farmers, local governments, and researchers with open tools, data, and insights to adapt to climate change and maximize agricultural efficiency.

Our vision is a highly resilient, data-driven agriculture sector in Nepal where decisions are backed by predictive intelligence and sustainable practice.

Key Research Areas

At AIRL, we prioritize research that has a direct, positive footprint on field outcomes. Our efforts span multi-disciplinary areas including:

  • Earth Observation & GIS: Utilizing multi-spectral satellite imagery to monitor crop health, vegetation indices, and soil properties across Nepal's diverse terrains.
  • Deep Learning for Phenotyping: Training mobile-friendly models to identify plant diseases, pests, and nutrient deficiencies instantaneously.
  • Climate-Smart Systems: Forecasting micro-climate adjustments to prepare farmers for seasonal anomalies and drought periods.

Our Core Values

The fundamental values that drive our lab’s culture and actions.

Scientific Integrity

We ensure rigorous research methodologies, reproducible data pipelines, and unbiased evaluations.

Open Innovation

All our core findings, software, and public datasets are shared openly under free licenses.

Farmer-Centricity

We design tools with low-resource environments and simple user experiences in mind.
