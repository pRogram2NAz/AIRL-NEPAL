Research Verticals

Three disciplines,
one mission

We approach smart farming from three interconnected scientific disciplines — building tools that work offline, at elevation, and in the hands of real farmers.

01 / IOT

IoT Telemetry Networks

Designing low-cost telemetry nodes tracking soil temperature, humidity, and chemical values in real time across distributed farm stations.

02 / GEO

Geodata Modeling

Processing Sentinel and Landsat imagery to forecast yield fluctuations and track agricultural land changes across Nepalese terrains.

03 / AI

Edge Diagnostic AI

Deploying optimized deep neural networks directly to lightweight mobile applications for offline crop disease diagnostics in the field.
